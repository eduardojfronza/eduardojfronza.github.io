Apple

15 maio 2022


Today I had a good experience with linguistic variation. Well, I was playing video games when my time mate said:

- Do you like “biscoito”

- I like it, but where I live it's called "bolacha"

We kept talking about the word differences in our states. He lives in Rio and I live in Rio Grande do Sul, we noticed that several words are spoken differently.

Today I was also reading the family group and I saw that there are several linguistic variations in the same group.
